Tribute to Ann Maina

Early Life

Ann Maina was born on the sixth day of the 10th month of the 1968th year AD.

She was born to William Gitonga and Sabina Wangari.The two were very excited for their first girl after having two sons.

She was a diligent daughter, always ready to help.

As she was the only daughter for a while, she had roles that were different from her brothers.

Till date, my grandmother always says she always felt at ease when Ann was home.

She studied in Rocho Girls and she always has a smile on her face when she speaks of her experience there.

Accomplishments

Ann is a very suucessful woman with many accomplishments but I still remember her tell me of when she first moved to Nairobi for University.

She was so proud of it that it should count as an achievement.I definitely see it as one.

She majored in Education and graduated from a masters in psychology around a decade ago.

She is passionate about teaching till date. You can see it in her eyes when she she sits with her grandchild and they read together.

I always say, not everyone can be a teacher.Not anyone has that kind of patience which is true because i know for a fact i wouldnt make a good teacher.

Due to this, being a teacher is an achievement in itself.

She then became a lecturer at a prestigious university and worked at another University during the weekends to take care of me.

Maybe she thinks I was too young to remember but I remember it all.

Being a sungle mum is not easy, but she is doing great so far and that should count as an achievement.

When I was in my last years in Primary school, she told me that she was up for a job as a Chief Education Officer.

You should have seen how happy she was just for the possibility that she could work in the field she's always wanted and also get a good paycheck.

I was so proud of her. Especially when she got the job.

She built her own house when In 2015 and bought her own car shortly afterwards.

Now, she works as a Sub County Director of Education and I always go around telling my friends what my mum does because i know how hard she has worked to get there.

Finale

I do not know if she knows just how grateful I am for everything she has done for me but I really am.

It was hard watching her juggle several jobs for my sake when I was younger.

It would have been easier for her if she had just asked me to stay with my dad when i told her i wanted to love with her.

She did not have to take me in.But she did.

I vowed to always make her proud and I might have slipped up abit in high school but i want to redeem myself here.

Nothing comes close to the happiness i felt after hearing my mum tell everyone how much i had passes in Primary.

She's turning 55 today.It's been 10 years since we started living together and I just want her to know I couldn't wish for a better mother.

Sometimes I annoy her and vice versa but bottom line is I would do anything for her and I apologise if I ever made it seem like i didnt appreciate her.

I promised her a house when I was younger and I still plan to deliver.
